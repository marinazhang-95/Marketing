Content Creation (Copywriting)

This is just a sample of me using HTML to prepare a simple description list, and ordered list. I recommend:

Always make a couple of sample tools and examples you can post to your website. Not only is it always beneficial to practice your markup skills, but it is also beneficial to show your potential clients exactly what you can do using HTML & CSS tools.

3 different types of samples you can create
  • 1.5 figure (1 figure with 0.5 caption)
  • 1 block quote example
  • 2 thematic breaks such as horizontal rules
Instructions
  1. Make an outline of what you want your sample to show. Plan it out in detail so you know exactly what you're talking about.
  2. Type up the raw text of everything you want to convey in your sample. Don't worry about HTML tags just yet, make sure all your information is clearly written out.
  3. Use HTML element tags to markup your ideas. Use a wide array of tags to best present your ideas. Here are some examples to help you get started: paragraphs, headings, lists, figures, etc.
